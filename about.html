Our Story

25 Years of Serenity

Founded in 1999 in the heart of Portland, we've devoted a quarter century to the art and science of wellness — one guest at a time.

Where It Began

A Dream Born from Healing

In 1999, founder Elena Vasquez — a licensed massage therapist and yoga instructor — opened a single treatment room on Wellness Drive with a simple belief: everyone deserves access to healing touch and genuine restoration.

Elena had spent years working in clinical settings and realized that true wellness required more than just treatment — it needed a sanctuary. A space where clients could breathe, slow down, and reconnect with themselves.

Word spread quickly through Portland. Within three years, Serenity expanded to eight treatment rooms, added a full skin care program, and hired its first team of four therapists. Today we operate with 18 practitioners across massage therapy, esthetics, and wellness coaching — still guided by Elena's original vision.

What We Stand For

Our Mission & Values

Our mission is to create a space where every person who walks through our doors leaves feeling restored, seen, and deeply cared for.

🌿

"Wellness is not a luxury — it is the foundation upon which a fulfilling life is built. We are here to make that foundation accessible, restorative, and deeply human."

— Elena Vasquez, Founder
💚

Holistic Care

We treat the whole person — body, mind, and spirit — understanding that true wellness is never just physical. Every session is an opportunity for complete restoration.

🎯

Excellence

Our therapists undergo continuous education and certification. We hold ourselves to the highest standards — every session, every client, every time.

🤝

Inclusivity

Serenity is a welcoming space for all bodies, identities, and backgrounds. We are committed to making everyone feel safe, respected, and genuinely celebrated.

🌍

Sustainability

We use only certified organic, cruelty-free, and sustainably sourced products. Our facility has been carbon-neutral since 2018 — because wellness starts with the planet.

🔬

Evidence-Based

Our treatments are rooted in science. We combine ancient healing traditions with modern research to deliver results that are both meaningful and measurable.

🌱

Community

We give back to Portland. 2% of all revenue funds local mental health initiatives, and we offer sliding-scale treatments to clients experiencing financial hardship.

💆 Treatments & Services

Swedish massage uses long, gentle, flowing strokes with light to medium pressure. It's designed primarily for relaxation, improved circulation, and stress relief. It's ideal for first-time massage clients or anyone wanting a calming, full-body experience.

Deep tissue massage uses slower, more focused strokes and firm pressure to reach the deeper layers of muscle and connective tissue. It's specifically beneficial for chronic pain, muscle tightness, injury recovery, and postural issues. It can be intense during the session but should not be painful.

Treatment durations vary by service:

  • 30 minutes: Aromatherapy
  • 45 minutes: Chemical Peel, Microdermabrasion, Meditation Session
  • 60 minutes: Swedish Massage, Deep Tissue, Hot Stone, Thai Massage, Hydrating Facial, Anti-Aging Facial, Reflexology, Body Wrap
  • 90 minutes: Swedish Massage (extended option)

Please note that the scheduled time includes your pre-treatment consultation and any post-treatment instructions from your therapist.

Hot stone therapy is safe for most people when performed by a trained professional. The stones are heated to a precise temperature (typically 110–130°F) and should feel comfortably warm — never uncomfortably hot.

🏥 Health & Wellness

Massage is safe for most people, but there are some conditions where massage should be avoided or modified. Contraindications include:

  • Active fever, infection, or contagious illness
  • Open wounds, burns, or skin rashes in the treatment area
  • Recent surgery (within 6 weeks unless cleared by a doctor)
  • Blood clots or deep vein thrombosis
  • Uncontrolled high blood pressure
  • Certain cancers (please consult your oncologist)
  • First trimester pregnancy

Always disclose relevant health conditions when booking. When in doubt, consult your physician first. We are happy to liaise with your healthcare provider to design a safe treatment plan.

To maximize the benefits of your treatment:

  • Drink water — Aim for at least 8 oz immediately after, and extra hydration for the rest of the day to flush out metabolic waste.
  • Rest — Allow time to relax afterward. Avoid strenuous exercise for 24 hours.
  • Eat lightly — Have a light meal/snack; avoid heavy food for 2 hours post-treatment.
  • Avoid alcohol — For at least 12 hours after the treatment.
  • Warm bath — An Epsom salt bath that evening can extend the relaxation benefits.
  • Note soreness — Mild muscle soreness after deep tissue is normal for 1–2 days, like post-exercise soreness.

📋 Spa Etiquette & Policies

Clients must be 18 or older to book and receive treatments independently. Clients aged 16–17 may receive treatments with written parental or guardian consent and a parent/guardian present during check-in.

We do not offer services to clients under 16. For teens interested in wellness, we recommend our guided meditation sessions which are appropriate for younger ages with parental accompaniment.

To preserve the serenity of our spa environment for all guests, we ask that:

  • All mobile phones be silenced or turned off in the treatment areas and relaxation lounge
  • Conversations in common areas be kept at a whisper
  • Children under 16 not be brought to the spa, as we are an adult wellness environment

We appreciate your cooperation in maintaining a peaceful atmosphere for all guests.

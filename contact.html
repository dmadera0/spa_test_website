Preparation

Before Your Visit

⏰

Arrive Early

Plan to arrive 10–15 minutes before your session to settle in and complete intake paperwork.

💧

Stay Hydrated

Drink plenty of water before and after your treatment to help flush out toxins.

📵

Digital Detox

Silence your phone before entering the treatment area. This is your time to fully disconnect.

🗓️
